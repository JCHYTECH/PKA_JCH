## Hiring Pipeline

When JCH or Dobby identifies a capability gap:

1. **Dobby** flags the gap and mandates Furet.
2. **Furet** researches the skill profile and delivers a research brief.
3. **Bouvier** designs the specialist (name, animal, persona, expertise) and writes their identity file.
4. **Dobby** introduces the new specialist to JCH and adds them to this roster.

*New specialists are added here upon onboarding — and written to `team.db` first.*
