### 🖼️ Style Analysis

From the examples:

- **Warm, golden tinted skin and fur** — enhanced ambers and rich earthy tones.

### Proposed Lightroom Classic Preset: **“MankeyPhoto Portrait”**

#### Basic Adjustments
- **Profile**: Adobe Color or Portrait
- **Temp**: +500 to +800 (warm amber cast)
- **Tint**: +10 to greenish balance
- **Exposure**: +0.2  
- **Contrast**: +30  
- **Highlights**: –40  
- **Shadows**: –20  
- **Whites**: +15  
- **Blacks**: –25  

#### Tone Curve
- **Medium Contrast**: Boost mid-high and drop shadows for depth.
- Slight “S‑curve” with lifted blacks (start at ~0,10) and boosted midtones (~75,80) and bright end (~95,98).

#### HSL / Color Adjustments
- **Reds & Oranges**: Hue +10; Saturation +15; Luminance +5  
- **Yellows**: Hue –5; Saturation +10  
- **Greens**: Desaturate (–20)  
- **Blues**: Hue shift toward teal (Hue –10), saturation –30, luminance –10  

#### Presence
- **Texture**: +20  
- **Clarity**: +15  
- **Dehaze**: +5  

#### Detail
- Sharpening: Amount 60, Radius 1.0, Detail 25  
- Noise Reduction: Luminance 10  

#### Effects
- **Vignette**: –15, midpoint ~50  
- **Grain**: Amount 10, size 25, roughness 50  

#### Calibration
- **Red Primary**: Hue +10, Sat +5  
- **Green Primary**: Hue –5, Sat –10  
- **Blue Primary**: Hue –5, Sat –20  

---

### 🎯 Why It Works

- **Warm amber cast** replicates that signature golden glow.
- **Deepened shadows plus mid-tone lift** deliver the high‑contrast, dramatic look.
- **Clarity and texture boosts** bring out each hair, whisker, and expressive eye.
- **HSL calibrations** emphasize fur tones and mute backgrounds appropriately.
- **Subtle grain and vignette** add filmic, focused character consistent with the source images.

---

### How to Fine‑Tune

- **Exposure**: Adjust slightly per photo.
- **White Balance**: Modify if lighting is cooler or bluer.
- **Shadows / Blacks**: Lighten if subject detail is lost.
- **Vibrance vs Saturation**: Use vibrance for more natural skin/fur tones.
- **Color Grade**: Add split-tone (warm highlights, cool shadows) if desired.
