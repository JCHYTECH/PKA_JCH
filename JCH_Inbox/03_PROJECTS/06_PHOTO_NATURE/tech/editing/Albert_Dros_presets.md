## ✅ Should You Use Them?

### **Why you might get them:**
- **Ready-made aesthetics:** save time applying a look similar to Dros'.
- **Beginner-friendly:** especially helpful if you're new to masking and color grading.
- **Structured tutorials:** some packs include video walkthroughs (e.g. for Lightroom and Photoshop workflows).

### **Things to consider:**
- **Not free**, so there’s a cost per pack.
- **Tweaking will be necessary**—you’ll still need to adjust sliders, masks, and exposure per shot.
- **They reflect Dros’ style**, which may not suit every scene or desired mood.

---

## 🛠️ How to Apply and Customize
1. **Download and install preset files (.xmp or .lrtemplate)** into Lightroom.
2. **Import them into Lightroom**, either via the Develop Module or a custom folder.
3. **Apply to an image**, then fine-tune:
   - White balance, exposure (highlights/shadows/blacks)
   - Local tones (sky/foreground) via mask tools
   - Color saturation and split toning (warm highlights, cool shadows)
4. **Optionally use Photoshop** for Orton-glow effects, luminosity masks, removing distractions, or dodge/burn layers -- techniques also taught in Dros’ courses citeturn0search3turn0search4.
